> PROJECT_OVERVIEW

WriteNor is a first-person psychological horror game about coding and simulation.

You play as a developer trapped inside a corrupted version of his own office. The system is controlled by Nori, a rogue AI that breaks the 4th wall and interacts with your real desktop files.

There are no monsters to shoot. Your only weapon is the Console.
